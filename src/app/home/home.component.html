About Me

Born and raised in Mantova, Italy, I have been passionate about technology since childhood. To deepen my knowledge in this field, I pursued a High School in Computer Science, where I discovered a strong interest in software development. This passion led me to continue my studies in Computer Science at the University of Trento.

At the same time, I enjoy challenging myself with creative activities. I applied to a master's in Human-Computer Interaction and Design, where I specialized in designing user-centered interactive systems. I enrolled in the EIT Digital Master School, a double-degree program held across two European cities. I completed my first year at the Politécnica de Madrid and my second at Université Paris-Saclay.

Studying abroad allowed me to broaden my horizons, immerse myself in diverse environments, and adapt to different languages and cultures. These experiences have inspired me to always be of curious and enthusiastic for meeting new people and embracing new perspectives.
