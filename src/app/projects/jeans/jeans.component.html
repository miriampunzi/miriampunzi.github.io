From: uxchallenge.co
Conducted open-ended interviews with 6 users (3 men, 3 women) to understand their online jeans shopping behavior.
  • Only 1 user successfully bought jeans online. All the others avoid this experience due to fear of poor fit, they prefer to try them in-store.
  • A user mentioned the problem of size inconsistency across brands.
  • 5 users answered they would be interested in using an avatar to preview the fit, even if they were unsure about its accuracy.
: users need a more visual, personalized, and trustworthy way to evaluate jeans fit online.

Application that helps users discover and select jeans that fit their unique body shape, while also providing expert advice and trend insights. The app aggregates jeans from multiple online providers, offering a personalized, educational, and confidence-boosting shopping experience.

Try the interactive prototype or explore the project on Figma
  • A discovery hub where users can explore new jeans, follow current trends, and read expert advice from image consultants.
  • Educational content explains body shapes, jeans types, and how center of gravity affects fit.
  • The user can visualize all the details related with a pair of jeans, including the bodyshapes that fit best
  • Embodiment with a personalized avatar allows users to visualize how jeans will fit their body, highlighting areas that may feel tight or loose.
  • Users can set up their profile customizing their avatar, their measurements, their style, and handle their account
  • The bodyshape and center of gravity are calculated based on the measurements provided
  • Measurement insertion using breadcrumbs UX principle
  • As part of a confidence-boosting experience, users are shown celebrities with similar body shapes and statistics about their body type
  • Users can select their style preferences, which will be used to personalize the jeans recommendations.
  • Targeted search experience that allows users to filter jeans by type (e.g., skinny, cropped, high-waist), brand, or body shape.
  • A personal space where users can save their favorite jeans to a wishlist or cart.
  • When ready, they're directed to the provider's platform to complete the purchase.
